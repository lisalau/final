Can you identify poison ivy?

Common Characteristics of Poison Ivy

Learn Ten Key Identifying Features:


1. Poison ivy usually grows on a hairy vine.

2. It may be found on the ground or scaling a tree.

3. The leaves appear slightly shiny.

4. The plant will fruit with white berries.

5. The middle leaflet will have a longer stem than the side leaflets.

6. The side leaflets will have a mitten shape.

7. Poison ivy leaf clusters alternate up the vine stem.

8. Poison ivy leaf clusters are not directly opposite each other.

9. Poison ivy may grow along the ground as a vine, or can grow as a shrub up to four feet tall.

10. Very old vines may develop a shaggy appearance against the tree.
